For students who want focused, one-on-one guidance beyond our free workshops. All services use sliding scale pricing — choose the tier that honestly reflects your situation.

Pricing tiers
Standard You're financially stable — employed, fully supported, no significant hardship.
Reduced Money is tight — you're a student, part-time worker, or managing on a limited budget. Limited spots monthly.
Low-income Genuine financial hardship — Pell Grant eligible or household income under $40k/year. Very limited spots monthly.
