Volunteer with
Bright Start.

Help us give homeschool students the tools and guidance they deserve. Both roles are part-time, flexible, and fully remote.

Part-time · Remote
Workshop Facilitator
Lead or co-facilitate weekly virtual workshops for homeschool students navigating the college process. You choose which sessions to sign up for from our rotating Mon–Thu schedule — no fixed weekly assignment.
Minimum commitment: 1 session per week
Part-time · Remote
Social Media Contributor
Create graphics, captions, and stories for our Instagram (@brightstartinitiative). Flexible schedule — you work when it suits you, as long as output stays consistent.
Minimum commitment: 3 posts per week
Who we're looking for

You don't need to be a college counselor or have a formal background in education. What matters most is that you genuinely care about helping students who are navigating the system without the same advantages others have.

Passionate about educational equity
Reliable and able to commit consistently
Comfortable speaking or presenting (for facilitators)
Creative with content or social media (for social contributors)
Homeschool background or experience is a plus, not required
