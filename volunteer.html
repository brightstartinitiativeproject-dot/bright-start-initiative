Join the team
As a BSI volunteer you'll help facilitate free group workshops for homeschool students on topics you know well. Sessions are held virtually on Zoom. You choose which topics you want to cover and how often you're available.
Please read before applying
Volunteers are unpaid. BSI is a free community initiative and all workshop facilitation is performed on a volunteer basis with no monetary compensation.
Volunteers facilitate group workshops only. All personalized 1-on-1 paid services are conducted exclusively by Ashley, the founder of BSI. Volunteers have no role in paid services under any circumstances.
Upon acceptance, you will receive a volunteer confirmation letter documenting your role, hours, and contributions — suitable for use on college applications, resumes, or scholarship materials.
I understand that this is a volunteer position with no monetary compensation, and that all paid 1-on-1 services at Bright Start Initiative are conducted exclusively by the founder, Ashley.
Thank you for applying! Ashley will review your application and follow up within one week.
