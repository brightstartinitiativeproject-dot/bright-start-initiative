Meet the founder

Hi, I'm Ashley.

I'm a homeschool graduate and first-year college student at Lamar University, where I conduct research on social capital differences between homeschool and elite preparatory school students.

I founded Bright Start Initiative because I know firsthand what it feels like to navigate college without a roadmap. I faced rejections from schools I had worked hard for — not because I wasn't capable, but because I didn't have access to the same resources and networks that traditional students did.

BSI exists so that no homeschool student has to figure it out alone. Every workshop here is free — because access shouldn't depend on where you went to school.

— Ashley
